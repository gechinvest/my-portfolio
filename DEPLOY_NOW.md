## 🎯 3 Simple Steps to Live (5 minutes)

### STEP 1: Get Supabase Keys (2 minutes)

1. Go to **https://supabase.com**
2. Create account or login
3. Click **"New Project"**
   - Name: `geta-tenaw-portfolio`
   - Pick a region
   - Set a password
4. Wait for it to load
5. Go to **Settings → API** (left sidebar)
6. Copy these values:
   - `VITE_SUPABASE_URL` = Your "Project URL"
   - `VITE_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY` = Your "anon public key"

✅ You'll use these in step 2!

---

### STEP 2: Deploy to Vercel (2 minutes)

1. Go to **https://vercel.com/new**
2. Log in with GitHub
3. Search for **`gechinvest/my-portfolio`**
4. Click **Import**
5. In **Environment Variables**:
   - `VITE_SUPABASE_URL` = (paste from step 1)
   - `VITE_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY` = (paste from step 1)
6. Click **"Deploy"** and wait 1-2 minutes

✅ Your portfolio is now LIVE! 🎉

---

### STEP 3: Customize & Verify (1 minute)

1. Go to your Vercel URL
2. Check it looks good
3. Go to `/admin` (e.g., `https://your-site.vercel.app/admin`)
4. Login with: `admin123`
5. Update your profile info
6. Edit skills, projects, experience
7. Change admin password to something secure!

✅ Done! Your portfolio is live and customized!
